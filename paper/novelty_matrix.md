# Novelty Matrix for the Temporal Sleipner Pivot

This matrix is the guardrail for the next paper. The gap we need to occupy is not "another pairwise Sleipner segmentation paper" and not "another inversion paper with a different regularizer." The clearest open space is the combination of:

- open public Sleipner data
- a multi-vintage pseudo-3D benchmark
- sequence-level inference across vintages
- held-out survey prediction and survey-value analysis

## Practical takeaway

- The strongest adjacent Sleipner papers are mainly about inversion, segmentation, or interpretation of pairwise 4D changes.
- The sequence-oriented papers are mainly about data matching or simulated reservoir workflows rather than open field sequence inference.
- The strongest safe novelty claim is therefore a public multi-vintage pseudo-3D Sleipner benchmark with temporal support assimilation and held-out survey evaluation.
